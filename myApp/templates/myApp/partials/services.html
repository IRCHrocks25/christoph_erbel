Retreats & Healing Journeys

Real presence. Deep integration.

Because some healing only happens in real presence.

There comes a moment when the screen is no longer enough. Your heart longs for a sacred circle. It wants to be felt, not just heard. A safe space to share what you feel deep inside. What obstacles you faced. Without shame or judgment. Just open and accepting hearts.

That's why I offer in-person retreats and transformational journeys. To come together, not just with me, but with others who walk a similar path.

These retreats follow the rhythm of the Selfhealing Path, but adapt to where you are.

We reconnect what's been lost or not fully integrated. We soften what's been hard. We bring clarity where confusion once ruled. And I guide you, not as someone above you, but beside you.

Common retreat topics, shaped by the real needs of women like you:

  • • Emotional overload and self-sabotage: how to stop abandoning yourself
  • • Shadow work: facing the parts you've been hiding, with compassion
  • • Relationship healing: from fear of rejection to honest connection
  • • Inner child and self-worth: becoming your own safe place
  • • Systemic family healing: releasing what was never yours to carry
  • • Purpose and direction: remembering why you're really here

"I've lost myself in all the roles I play. But here, I can just be me, without judgment, that changes everything."

Healing Journeys: Sacred Lands, Sacred Work

Some healing needs more than a weekend. It needs space. Stillness. Sacred ground. Time.

That's why I invite small groups of women and men to Peru and Bolivia. Not for sightseeing, but for soul travel. Not to escape your life, but to meet yourself.

Here's what happens:

  • • 10+ days of continuous guidance & integration
  • • Ancient healing ceremonies in ancient temples and sacred nature
  • • Optional plant medicine rituals (always voluntary, deeply respectful)
  • • One-to-one attention, deep group sharing, and real breakthroughs

These journeys are intense, and deeply transformative. You'll feel held by the land, the energy, the group, and by me. It's the next step that requires preparation. But if your soul is ready, you'll know.
