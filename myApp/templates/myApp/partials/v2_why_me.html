The values I live by.

For me, it was always important that the teachers I learned from shared the same values I believe in. And that is why I want you to know what kind of attitude I live by.

LOVE

When I chose love, real unconditional love, something inside me shifted. False identities fell away. I began to feel whole again. Connected. Alive. At peace.

GRATITUDE

I already have everything I need to live my life — not perfectly, but truthfully. In my own rhythm. Gratitude has become my daily beginning.

FORGIVENESS

Forgiveness doesn’t mean excusing what happened. It means choosing not to carry the pain anymore. It didn’t change the past. But it changed me. And that changed everything.

AWARENESS

The only place where healing and transformation can happen is here. Now. In this breath.

TRUST

You don’t need to trust the whole path. Just the next step. And then the next. That’s how I learned to trust again.

COURAGE

Sometimes courage looks like showing up, even with shaking hands. Like saying yes to yourself when everything inside you says stay safe.

SELF-RESPONSIBILITY

No one can walk your path for you. But here’s the difference. You don’t have to walk it alone, like I did. I’m here to offer you what I once longed for.
